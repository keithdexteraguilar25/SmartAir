Safety instructions
please read carefully.

Reminder 1

This device is not intended for use by persons (including children) with reduced physical, sensory or mental capabilities. It is also not intended for use by those with a lack of experience and knowledge, unless they have been given supervision or instruction concerning the use of the appliance by a person responsible for their safety.

Reminder 2

Never operate this appliance if the cord or power supply are damaged. Ensure the power cord is not stretched or exposed to sharp objects/edges.

Reminder 3

Any service other than regular cleaning or filter replacement should be performed by an authorised service representative. Failure to comply could result in a voided warranty.

Reminder 4

The use of accessory attachments that have not been approved by the appliance manufacturer is not recommended, as this may result in fire, cause an electric shock and/or cause personal injury.

Reminder 5

Only place essential oils within the diffuser unit. Allowing the oils to come into contact with other parts of the appliance may result in damage.

Reminder 6

Never use the power supply as a switch to start and turn off the air purifier. Use the provided ON/OFF button located on the control panel.
